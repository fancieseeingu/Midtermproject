My Comfort Food is Banana Cue

Ingredients:

  • saba bananas
  • brown sugar
  • water
  • cooking oil

Step How to Make Banana Cue:

  1. Peel and Slice the banana into half.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the bananas and fry for about 2 minutes until they start to soften and lightly brown.
  4. Sprinkle the brown sugar directly into the hot oil and let it sit for a moment to caramelize
  5. Stir gently to coat bananas in caramelized sugar, then fry until crisp
  6. Remove the banana cue from the oil and drain the excess oil using strainer.
  7. Lastly, serve it warm and enjoy!
